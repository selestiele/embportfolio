About Me

Hi! I'm a creative person with technophile leanings. You can usually find me with an eReader, playing with my kids, or trying to keep my kitten from destroying all my curtains. If I'm feeling frisky, I'll do all three, while simultaneously dancing to a song of Alexa's choice. When I'm not doing one of those things, I enjoy cooking, writing, and falling down the rabbit hole of internet research. There is so much amazing information out there, and I love learning. YouTube and blogs have taught me how to use Photoshop, Illustrator, make cream puffs, build furniture, and a number of other interesting skills.

I recently decided to go back to school to fill gaps in my web development knowledge. While I love to create, I also appreciate the less subjective side of coding. My end goal is to work as a front-end web developer, bringing aesthetic beauty and intutitive functionality to clients' websites.

I also design book covers and marketing materials for independent authors and small publishers under the name Selestiele Designs. You can view my author-centered portfolio on the Selestiele Designs website.
